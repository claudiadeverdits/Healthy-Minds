How Can You Help?


- Know the warning signs — you can learn more under "Educate Yourself".

- Express your concern and support with words like “I” and “we”.

- Learn more about mental health! The more you know, the better you can empathize and understand others.

- Remind them that mental health problems can be treated. Bring up the idea of getting treatment and seeking real help.

- Be empathetic, patient, and respectful. Avoid coming off as judgemental.

- Listen to what they have to say.

- Have realistic expectations — recovery is not instant.

- As you help others, make sure to also take proper care of yourself.
