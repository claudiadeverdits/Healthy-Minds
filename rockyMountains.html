Rocky Mountains Mental Health Resources and Treatment

Please remember: If any of the phone numbers don't work and you are in an emergency, call or text the national lines on the right and don't stop trying until you get the help you need!

Colorado

NAMI

Hotline: Call Colorado Crisis Services at 1-844-493-8255.

Idaho

NAMI

Hotline: Call or text the Idaho Suicide Prevention and Crisis Line at 208-398-4357.
