Please remember: If any of the phone numbers don't work and you are in an emergency, call or text the national lines on the right and don't stop trying until you get the help you need!

New Mexico

NAMI

Hotline: Call the New Mexico Crisis and Access Line at 855-662-7474

Warmline: Call the Peer to Peer warmline at 855-266-7100.

Oklahoma

NAMI

Hotline: Call the 24 hour hotline at 1-800-522-9054.

You can also call the NAMI Oklahoma Helpline at 1-800-583-1264 for mental health info and support. (This line is available M-F, 9am-4pm.)
