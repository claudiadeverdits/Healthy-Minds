Midwest Mental Health Resources and Treatment

Please remember: If any of the phone numbers don't work and you are in an emergency, call or text the national lines on the right and don't stop trying until you get the help you need!

Illinois

NAMI

Hotline: Text TALK to the Illinois Call4Calm Text Line at 552020 for English or HABLAR for Spanish.

Indiana

NAMI

Hotline: Call the Indiana 211 line or text your zip code to 898-211 (available M-F, 8am-5pm)

Iowa

NAMI

Hotline: Call Your Life Iowa at 1-855-581-8111 or text 1-855-895-8398.

Kansas

NAMI

Hotline: Use this resource to find 24-hour hotlines by county.

Michigan

NAMI

Hotline: Use this map to find hotlines by county.

Minnesota

NAMI

Hotline: Call NYC Well at 1-888-692-9355 or text WELL to 65173.

Missouri

NAMI

Hotline: Use this resource to find hotlines by county.

Nebraska

NAMI

Hotline: Call the national suicide line (1-800-273-8255) if you are in an emergency.

North Dakota

MHA

Hotline: Use this resource to find a hotline near you.

Ohio

NAMI

Hotline: Call 800-686-2646 or the Careline emotional support line at 800-720-9616.

South Dakota

NAMI

Hotline: Call 2-1-1 for the Helpline Center.

Wisconsin

NAMI

Hotline: Use this resource to find a hotline near you.
