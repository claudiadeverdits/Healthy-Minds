About Us!


We are a group of high school and college girls who wanted to use our passion for computer science to help those struggling with their mental health. Mental health is an extremely prevalent aspect of our society that affects a large and diverse part of the population. Despite this, there is a lot of stigma about the topic and many peoples' issues are overlooked. Determined to do something about it, we created Healthy Minds, a website filled with resources about mental health. Read below to learn more about each of us:
